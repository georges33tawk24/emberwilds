EMBERWILDS — a free warm-pixel action platformer

EMBERWILDS is a free, hand-crafted action platformer you play right in your browser — no download, no sign-up, on desktop and mobile. Play Sorrel, a quick red fox with a spring-loaded slingblaster, and relight the Warmth Beacons across a world being drained of its warmth by the industrial Rust and its master, Baron Coglar.

Explore three hand-crafted worlds

  • Thornwood — sunlit forests that teach you to run, jump, stomp, and glide.
  • Ochre Canyon — autumn desert mesas built for speed, springs, and momentum.
  • Mossgrave Ruins — a drowned temple of swimming, keys, and switch-gate puzzles.

Every level is a Super-Mario-sized sprawl with a safe low road, a fast high road, and hidden secret rooms — plus four hidden Ember Tokens to find and multi-phase bosses to outsmart.

How to play

Move with the arrow keys or WASD, jump with Z or Space (hold to jump higher, or hold while falling to glide), shoot with X, and ground-pound with C. On phones and tablets, thumb-friendly touch controls appear automatically — just turn to landscape and tap to play.

Features

  • Free to play in any modern browser — installable as an offline app (PWA).
  • Tight, momentum-driven controls: coyote time, jump buffering, glide, wall-jump, ground-pound.
  • Mario-style power-up transformations: spread shots, piercing fire, freeze-platforms, and a hover glide.
  • Hand-authored pixel art, original chiptune music, and a warm, no-neon art style.
  • Mobile-first: full-screen support and controls sized for thumbs.
