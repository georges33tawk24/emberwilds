# Privacy Policy — Emberwilds

_Last updated: 2026-07-10._

This policy explains what data Emberwilds ("the Game", "we") handles. It is
written to satisfy GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and general international
expectations. Emberwilds is a browser game with no account system and collects
no personally identifying information beyond an optional display name you choose
yourself.

> **Contact:** contact@emberwilds.fun

## What we store

**On your device (localStorage).** Your progress, best times, unlocked
cosmetics, achievements, settings, and a randomly generated anonymous device id.
This never leaves your device except as described below. Clearing your browser
storage erases it.

**On our leaderboard (when you set a clear-time record).** Your chosen display
name, your clear time, the level, and the anonymous device id — sent to a
first-party Cloudflare Worker so global leaderboards work. No IP address, email,
or account is stored with it. Pick any display name you like; you are not
required to use a real one.

## Analytics

The self-hosted version at **emberwilds.fun** uses **Google Analytics 4** to
count anonymous gameplay events (game started, level started/completed/failed,
session length). This helps us see where players get stuck. GA4 may set cookies
and process data per [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
We do not send GA4 any name or identifier you provide.

## Advertising

The **emberwilds.fun** build may show ads via **Google AdSense**, which can use
cookies to serve and measure ads per Google's policies. Ads are **not** shown
during active gameplay.

When you play Emberwilds **on a game portal** (CrazyGames, Poki, Yandex Games,
GameDistribution, etc.), advertising, analytics, and any cloud save are provided
by **that portal's own SDK**, governed by that portal's privacy policy:

- CrazyGames — https://www.crazygames.com/privacy
- Poki — https://poki.com/en/c/privacy-policy
- Yandex Games — https://yandex.com/legal/confidential/
- GameDistribution — https://gamedistribution.com/privacy-policy/

## Cookies & tracking

- **Self-hosted (emberwilds.fun):** cookies from Google Analytics and, if shown,
  Google AdSense. Game progress uses localStorage, not cookies.
- **On portals:** any cookies/tracking are set by the portal SDK, not by us.

## Children

Emberwilds is family-friendly and contains no user-to-user communication. On
portals, ad delivery to minors is handled by the portal per COPPA/GDPR-K.

## Your rights

Because we hold no account and no contact data, there is nothing to look up by
identity. You can:

- **Delete your local data** — clear your browser storage for the site.
- **Remove a leaderboard entry** — email the contact above with the display name
  and level; we will remove it.
- Under GDPR/CCPA you may request deletion of the leaderboard record tied to your
  device id.

## Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date reflects the latest version.
