- ✅ Engagement events: `settings_changed`, `character_selected`, `level_replayed`
- ✅ Monetization events: `ad_requested`, `interstitial_shown`, `reward_claimed`, `ad_failed` (fired centrally by `AdManager`)

## Monetization
- ✅ Platform SDK abstraction (`src/platform/`) — ads/save routed through one façade
- ✅ `AdManager` (`src/systems/adManager.ts`) — one entry point for ads, analytics-instrumented
- ✅ Interstitial wired between levels (`ClearScene.advance()`) — no-op off-portal, natural break on portals
- ✅ `AdManager.showRewarded()` ready + `rewardedAvailable()` gate. **Design decision pending:** what the reward is (the game uses checkpoints, not lives, so "continue after death" doesn't fit — a "double ember-gems" offer on the clear screen fits the economy). Wire the call once chosen.
