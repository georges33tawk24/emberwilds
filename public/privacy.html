Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-10

This policy explains what data Emberwilds ("the Game", "we") handles. It is written to satisfy GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and general international expectations. Emberwilds is a browser game with no account system — it collects no personally identifying information beyond an optional display name you choose yourself.

What we store

  • On your device (localStorage): your progress, best times, unlocked cosmetics, achievements, settings, and a randomly generated anonymous device id. Clearing your browser storage erases it.
  • On our leaderboard (when you set a clear-time record): your chosen display name, your clear time, the level, and the anonymous device id — sent to a first-party Cloudflare Worker so global leaderboards work. No IP address, email, or account is stored with it. Pick any display name you like.

Analytics

The self-hosted version at emberwilds.fun uses Google Analytics 4 to count anonymous gameplay events (game started, level started/completed/failed, session length), so we can see where players get stuck. GA4 may set cookies and process data per Google's privacy policy — but only after you accept the consent banner; until then analytics cookies are disabled (Google Consent Mode v2). We never send GA4 any name or identifier you provide.

Advertising

The emberwilds.fun build may show ads via Google AdSense, which can use cookies to serve and measure ads per Google's policies — likewise only after consent. Ads are never shown during active gameplay.

When you play Emberwilds on a game portal, advertising, analytics, and any cloud save are provided by that portal's own SDK, governed by that portal's privacy policy:

  • CrazyGames — crazygames.com/privacy
  • Poki — poki.com/en/c/privacy-policy
  • Yandex Games — yandex.com/legal/confidential
  • GameDistribution — gamedistribution.com/privacy-policy

Cookies & tracking

  • Self-hosted (emberwilds.fun): cookies from Google Analytics and, if shown, Google AdSense — both gated behind the consent banner. Game progress uses localStorage, not cookies.
  • On portals: any cookies/tracking are set by the portal's SDK, not by us.

Children

Emberwilds is family-friendly and contains no user-to-user communication. On portals, ad delivery to minors is handled by the portal per COPPA / GDPR-K.

Your rights

Because we hold no account and no contact data, there is nothing to look up by identity. You can:

  • Delete your local data — clear your browser storage for this site.
  • Remove a leaderboard entry — email us the display name and level, and we will remove it.
  • Under GDPR/CCPA, request deletion of the leaderboard record tied to your device id.
